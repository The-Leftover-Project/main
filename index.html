Our Simple, Effective Process

We make it easy to get good food to people who need it most.

1. Restaurants Donate

We partner with local restaurants, cafes, and stores to save their delicious, unsold surplus food.

2. We Collect & Deliver

Our amazing volunteers spring into action, collecting the food and delivering it safely and quickly.

3. Community is Fed

We deliver the meals to local food banks and shelters, helping families, children, and seniors in our community.

Our Story is About Community

The Leftover Project was born from a simple observation: good food was being thrown away while our neighbors were going hungry. We knew we had to act. Our mission is driven by compassion, responsibility, and a genuine desire to make a difference.

"Every meal we share helps families, children, and seniors stay healthy and hopeful. It's more than just food; it's a message that someone cares."
